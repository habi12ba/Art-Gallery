The 15th edition of the India Art Fair

Attention All Art Enthusiasts!

The long-awaited India Art Fair 2024 has finally arrived, offering an immersive experience into South Asia's contemporary art scene. Make sure to mark your calendars, brush up on your art knowledge, and get ready to be amazed! It's time for the curtains to open as art connoisseurs from India and abroad head to South Delhi to witness the 15th edition of the India Art Fair.

Timing: 10am

Dans La Lumière

Calling All Art Lovers!

Perrotin Paris presents Dans La Lumière, an exhibition delving into CHIROPTERA, the monumental ballet by JR, Damien Jalet, and Thomas Bangalter. The exhibition features JR's Back to the Cavern photographic series, with two installations: a cave entrance and an interior with handprint-covered walls. Additionally, Aletheia 19, a sound installation by Thomas Bangalter, runs until June 15th. This immersive blend of visual and auditory art promises a captivating experience.

Timing: 10am

The Harlem Renaissance and Transatlantic Modernism

Dear Art Lovers,

Years in the making, the highly anticipated show The Harlem Renaissance and Transatlantic Modernism reflects the Met's efforts to place the work of Black artists in a new light. Expect works by Harlem Renaissance artists including Charles Alston, Archibald Motley, and James Van Der Zee - largely drawn from the collections of historically Black colleges and universities (HBCUs) - to hang alongside those of European contemporaries such as Henri Matisse, Edvard Munch, and Pablo Picasso.

Timing: 11am
